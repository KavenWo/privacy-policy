Privacy Policy for Dayly

Last updated: July 18, 2025

The Simple Version

Dayly is a personal journaling app that keeps your thoughts private. Your journal entries, mood data, and personal information stay on your device only. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information with anyone.

What Information We Handle

Your Journal Content:

  • Journal entries you write
  • Mood selections you make
  • Dates and times when you create entries

Technical Information:

  • Device screen dimensions (to display the app properly)
  • Basic device information (for app functionality)
  • App usage timestamps
Important: All this information stays on your device. We don't send it anywhere or store it on our servers.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect your personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • We don't send your data to servers or the cloud
  • We don't share your information with anyone
  • We don't use analytics or tracking
  • We don't show ads or use your data for marketing

Your Data, Your Control

Since everything is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete any entry anytime
  • Uninstalling the app removes all your data
  • No one else can access your journal entries
  • You don't need to create an account

Future Updates

As Dayly grows, we may add features like:

  • Optional cloud backup (with your permission)
  • User accounts (optional)
  • Analytics to improve the app
  • AI-powered insights

If we add these features, we'll update this privacy policy and let you know. You'll always have the choice to use them or not.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when we add new features. We'll notify you of any changes through the app or by posting the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal data, there's not much to manage! But you always have the right to:

  • Delete your data (by deleting entries or uninstalling)
  • Contact us with questions
  • Stop using the app anytime

This privacy policy is designed to be simple and honest about what Dayly actually does. As we add features, we'll update this policy to keep it accurate and transparent.
